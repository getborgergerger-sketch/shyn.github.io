Words I've Been Afraid to Say

I've been thinking about you a lot lately.
More than I probably should admit.
Every quiet moment, you're there.
I think... I might be falling for you.
I remember that night we stayed up talking until 3am. You laughed at my terrible jokes, and I felt like I could tell you anything. Since then, everything reminds me of you - the songs on my playlist, the coffee I make every morning, even the way the sunlight hits my window.

I'm scared to say this because I don't want to ruin what we have. But I can't keep pretending I don't feel this pull toward you. You're the first person I want to tell when something good happens. The one I think about when I'm falling asleep.

I don't know if you feel the same, but I had to be honest. These are the words I've been afraid to say... I love you.
